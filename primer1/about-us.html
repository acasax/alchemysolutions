About Us

Alchemy Solutions is a consulting agency with many years of experience in the field of business consulting.

MISSION STATEMENT
Providing effective consultancy to our business partners by optimising their processes and resources.
VISION
Becoming reliable partner for business processes consultancy and implementation of continuous improvement methodology.

Our team has vast experience in the field of business consulting. Alchemy Solutions advises both public and private sector clients helping them improve and refine their business models, take them to the next level, and emerge as leaders in their fields. We are focused on an innovative, dynamic, and effective approach that guarantees results.

Our areas of expertise are:

1. Operational excellence
- Process optimizations and debottlenecking - Lean 6S Black Belt Certified
- CapEx (Capital projects management) - PMP certified
- Planning efficiency - a novel model for planning process evaluation and improvement

2. Organizational efficiency:
- Leadership development
- Strategic thinking and planning
- Engineering reorganization
- Coaching and training

Thanks to years of experience and dedication, we have achieved many successes in the field of business consulting.
